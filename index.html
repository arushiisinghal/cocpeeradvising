Undergraduate Peer Advising

The College of Chemistry Undergraduate Peer Advising program provides undergraduates with advice regarding selecting courses, designing a balanced workload, obtaining research, and developing an academic plan.

Peer advisors hold weekly advising hours and are located in 121 Gilman Hall. Students are encouraged to visit during peak enrollment periods.

Contact: cocpeeradviser@berkeley.edu

Peer Advising Covalent Program

The Covalent Program pairs incoming students with peer mentors to support academic exploration and personal development. Mentees participate in weekly sessions focused on goal-setting, research pathways, and academic success.
